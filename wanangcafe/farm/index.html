Farm stay in the Thai countryside

Venture just outside Bangkok to learn about farming and rural Thai culture.

From there, we’ll take a long-tail boat to visit ancient Buddhist temples, head back to the farm, change into traditional farmer clothes, and make Thai herb drink. Next we’ll bike to rice fields and fish farms, then head back for a dinner cooked by the host’s mom.

We’ll fall asleep on handmade Thai beds and wake up the next morning to see ceramics being crafted in the famous Porcelain Village. Read more...

Organic California farm stay

Explore a small family farm, pick organic fruit and bake a perfect dessert

Guests will arrive at the farm in the early afternoon and be shown to their lodging in one of 4 private rooms.

After registering and settling in, the guests are free to relax from their journey and explore the farm. Guests will then go out to enjoy the local cuisine which can range from formal dining to taco truck fare.. Read more...

Douro Valley Grape Treading & Harvest

Tread on grapes and be part of the harvest in a Douro Valley Wine farm.

Embark on a sensorial five day adventure to the Douro Valley that will include a traditional grape harvest, treading on the grapes, wine making workshops, 3 wine tastings, 15 traditional meals, a visit to UNESCO prehistoric rock art sites & museum, a Douro river boat journey as well as the shipment of 6 bottles of the wine you helped to make to your home address.
